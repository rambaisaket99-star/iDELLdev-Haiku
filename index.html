FAQ

Common Questions

Is iDELL Haiku safe for my account?

Yes. We never ask for your password. All services are delivered from external sources without any risk to your account. Payments are done through UPI, completely secure.

How fast is the delivery?

Most orders start within 1-5 minutes. Delivery time depends on the quantity ordered. You can track your order live from the dashboard.

What if my follower count drops?

All refill-guaranteed services will be automatically topped up at no extra cost. Check the refill policy column in our pricing table.

How do I add balance to my account?

Go to Add Funds in your dashboard, scan our PhonePe QR code, enter the amount, and your balance will be updated manually within a few minutes after verification.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, and any UPI payment. Bank transfer is also accepted for larger orders above Rs. 500.
